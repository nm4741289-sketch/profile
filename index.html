Case Study 1

About The Content

A modest fashion brand selling hijabs, with a decent posting routine but limited clarity in profile setup

The Challenges

The profile lacked keywords in the name, bio was too simple, and highlights/captions were not optimized for conversion. The feed looked inconsistent, which weakened brand identity

My Audit & Recommendations

Add niche keywords in the name for discoverability.

Refine bio with a clear offer line

Use a consistent 2–3 color palette for brand identity

Balance content mix (products + styling tips + customer reviews)

Add CTAs in captions and stories.

Improve highlight covers & use trendy audios for better reach

Result & Takeaway

Clarity in bio and consistent branding can instantly upgrade trust. Even small changes like keywords and CTA captions lead to higher profile visits and stronger engagement

Case Study 2

About The Content

A creative stationery brand with 11k+ followers, offering journals, sketchbooks, and doodle art.

The Challenges

Despite a large follower base, engagement was low. Bio looked like a catalogue, highlights were cluttered (100+), and captions lacked emotional connection.

My Audit & Recommendations

Reframe bio with a clear, emotional message + shop link.

Reduce highlights to 5–6 core categories

Build a posting schedule (3–4 posts + 2–3 reels per week)

Add educational/inspirational posts to balance heavy product focus

Push BTS/process content (audience loves “how it’s made”)

Strengthen captions with storytelling and CTAs

Mix niche + branded hashtags for better reach

Result & Takeaway

Even with 11k followers, engagement dies without storytelling and community focus. A structured strategy turns a product-heavy feed into an interactive, trust-building brand presence

Case Study 3

About The Content

A new personal brand account focused on content creation, design, and branding. Fresh start with almost no consistent posting

The Challenges

Branding wasn’t aligned, highlights were random/personal, and feed had only one post. The bio was creative but unclear for new visitors

My Audit & Recommendations

Refine bio with clear brand promise + CTA

Fix 2–3 brand colors and consistent highlight covers

Create 3 content pillars (Educational, Portfolio, Personal storytelling)

Start with 3 posts per week using simple Canva templates

Write captions in problem–solution–CTA style

Use targeted hashtags (8–12 per post)

Add audience-focused polls/Q&As in stories

Result & Takeaway

A fresh account can be a blessing — with the right brand identity, content pillars, and posting consistency, even new pages can quickly look professional and attract clients
